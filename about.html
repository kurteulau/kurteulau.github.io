Before developing a passion for data science, I was a UC Berkeley undergraduate student and then a Peace Corps Volunteer in Cambodia. After Peace Corps, I taught math and computer science in Title I public schools in New York City and California for seven years. Teaching math and computer science motivated me to explore the nexus of those two disciplines: data science. You can now find me completing the UC Berkeley Master of Information and Data Science program as a graduate student while improving the capacity of solar energy companies as an analyst with Catalyst Off-Grid Advisors.

In my spare time I enjoy hangning out with my spouse, playing boardgames, surfing, reading, and exploring the outdoors.
